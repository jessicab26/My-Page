Traveling through Photography

I love to travel! Anywhere, anytime, any place! I've been to 11 states within the Continental US, and I plan to see more!


Along the way, I've gathered quite the photo collection. Although I do not take photos professionally, I do consider it to be my hobby! I love capturing the memories of places I've visited. Each picture is a small reminder of places I've visited and the beauty that is out there!
