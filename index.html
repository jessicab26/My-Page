Traveling through Photography

Hello!

My name is Jessica Brogden. And. . . I love to travel! Anywhere, anytime, any place! I love the "getting there" and everything in between. Although the trip home is not always my favorite, it is nice to make it back to my bed.

So far, I've been to 11 states within the Continental US. Though it may not seem like many, my car has the miles to show for it! And along the way, I've gathered quite the photo collection. Although I do not take photos professionally, I do consider it to be my hobby! I love capturing the memories of places I've visited. Each picture is a small reminder of the beauty that is out there!
